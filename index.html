Who is zfronk?

Zfronk is more than just his name, it's his personal journey.

Wanting to spend time away from screens with a love for food, Steve Aboll opened a fast food joint, which he had no idea could blow, as it is today.

What we serve

We specialize in authentic African street food cooked at just the right temperature, seasoned with tradition, and served with pride. From sizzling nyama choma to Zfronk's secret samaki from the lake, combod with some sukuma, We can never fail to brighten your mood.
